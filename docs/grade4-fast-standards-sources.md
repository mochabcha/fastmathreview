# Grade 4 FAST Math Standards Sources

The benchmark coverage for this project follows the official Florida FAST mathematics blueprint for grade 4.

Primary sources used on May 11, 2026:

1. Florida Department of Education FAST assessments page
   `https://www.fldoe.org/accountability/assessments/k-12-student-assessment/best/`

2. Official FAST mathematics test design summary and blueprint
   `https://flfast.org/content/contentresources/en/Test-Design-Summary---FAST-Mathematics_10112022.pdf`

3. Florida mathematics standards hub
   `https://www.fldoe.org/academics/standards/subject-areas/math-science/mathematics/`

Implementation note:

- The benchmark codes and reporting-category percentages in `src/content/standards/` reflect the official blueprint.
- The short student-facing standard summaries in the app are paraphrases for readability, not verbatim standard text.
- The imported 50-question seed covers most of the blueprint directly. The standards catalog also includes `MA.4.NSO.2.1` and `MA.4.AR.1.2` because those appear in the official blueprint coverage even though they are not present in the supplied PDF review bank.
